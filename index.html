TOP 5 FAVORITE MOVIES

1. AVENGERS: END GAME | Made by: Anthony and Joe Russo | Published on: April 22, 2019 |
This movie is the sequel of the movie Avengers Infinity War. In the second part, Thanos almost has all the infinity stones and the avengers are trying to stop him before he snaps which causes half of the world to die.
2. BLACK ADAM | Made by: C. C. Beck and Otto Binder | Published on: October 3 2022 |
A girl frees a super hero who is somewhat bad but turns better throughout the movie to save the world from a prophecy.
3. BACK INTO THE FUTURE | Made by: Robert Zemeckis | Published on: July 3, 1985 |
A kid named Marty McFly is transported back in time after an experiment has gone horribly wrong and has to find a way to get back while meeting younger versions of people he knows.
4. SONIC THE HEDGEHOG MOVIE | Made by: Jeff Fowler | Published on: January 25, 2020 |
Sonic used one of his rings to go to earth where he could try and take a break from Dr Robotnik, but when he cause a mass power outage, this caught the eye of Robotnik who now is trying to steal his power.
5. THE MAN FROM TORONTO | Made by: Patrick Hughes | Published on: June 24, 2022 |
A guy accidently arrives in the wrong location for his vacation and is thought to be the Man from Toronto who is a skilled assasin. Now after the real Man from Toronto found out about this identity case, both the real and fake Man from Tornto have to work together since most people now know the fake Man from Toronto as the real one.
